About me

Aaron Lawrence

Developer & Business Analyst

I am a designer, developer, product manager and business analyst. I have worked in the mortgage industry for the last 15 years and am very fortunate to have a job I love. I am a problem solver who thrives on a good challenge. I got my start building custom loan origination software that has funded billions over it’s 10 year run. My passion is creating solutions that leverage technology to solve business needs.

I am comfortable wrangling HTML, Javascript and CSS code to make beautiful websites that work on any platform, managing complex projects with tight deadlines or hashing out the intricate details of RegB, TRID or HMDA to insure mortgage operations stay compliant with the ever changing laws and regulations. I have worked many positions in the mortgage industry along with developing software and this has given me the skills needed to craft solutions that make people's lives easier and bring financial success to the companies I have worked for.

When I am not solving the the ever changing challenges of the mortgage industry, you will find me photographing my surrounding city’s buildings, bridges, tunnels and architectural wonders. Photography is a passion I found several years ago through the tiny lens of my mobile phone and I cannot imagine my life without it. Art has also been my passion since I was young and I love drawing or doodling what ever crazy shapes and images are floating through my brain. It is my release from the rigid and compliance driven world of the mortgage industry.

Loan Origination System

 

A custom Loan Origination system built for a mortgage lender to fulfill all of their operations processes from initial application submission through post closing. Utilized technologies: .Net, Classic ASP, SQL Server, jQuery and Bootstrap. A custom reporting engine was built using Crystal Reports.

Rate Sheet Publishing

 

A sophisticated mortgage rate sheet publishing platform. This tool allowed raw pricing to be imported, margins applied, adjustment management and publishing of HTML and PDF versions of daily rate sheets. Historical pricing archives allowed easy repricing, extensions or product changes without manual intervention. Eliminated countless hours in daily management of numerous Excel based rate sheets.

Mortgage Trade Best Execution

 

A mortgage trade best execution utility that imported bulk pricing bids from numerous investors and analyzed each loan for it's best pricing. Clients saw immediate gains of $40-50k per month in increased trade revenue and drastically streamlined the bidding process.
