Loan Origination System

 

A custom Loan Origination system built for a mortgage lender to fulfill all of their operations processes from initial application submission through post closing. Utilized technologies: .Net, Classic ASP, SQL Server, jQuery and Bootstrap. A custom reporting engine was built using Crystal Reports.

Rate Sheet Publishing

 

A sophisticated mortgage rate sheet publishing platform. This tool allowed raw pricing to be imported, margins applied, adjustment management and publishing of HTML and PDF versions of daily rate sheets. Historical pricing archives allowed easy repricing, extensions or product changes without manual intervention. Eliminated countless hours in daily management of numerous Excel based rate sheets.

Mortgage Trade Best Execution

 

A mortgage trade best execution utility that imported bulk pricing bids from numerous investors and analyzed each loan for it's best pricing. Clients saw immediate gains of $40-50k per month in increased trade revenue and drastically streamlined the bidding process.

Borrower Mobile App

 

A native iOS and Android mobile app supporting borrower's seeking to obtain a mortgage loan or customers making payments on their mortgage. The mobile app allowed borrowers track their loan status, upload documents, apply for a loan and make payments.

Loan Officer Mobile App

 

A native iOS and Android mobile app supporting Loan Officers in the field. This app provided critical real-time status updates, approval information and contacts. In addition an automated pricing an eligibility engine was built to price and lock loans while on the go.

Online Loan Application

 

An web based mortgage loan application process that allowed borrowers to apply and track the status of thier loan application. Tasks and document uploads allowed borrowers to provide needed data in real-time.

HMDA Review/Audit Tracking Form

 

A custom Encompass form that allows compliance staff to review and audit all aspects of HMDA data. All necessary data is in one place, saving valuable time during audits. Each data elenment that is reviewed gets logged along with notes for reporting purposes. HMDA data values are shown in both native and HMDA specific formats, allowing staff to verify the accuracy of data conversion before submission to regulatory agencies.

Compliance Review Tracking Form

 

A custom compliance review tracking form that gave compliance staff a central place to review and log all compliance review activites. This included disclosures, RegB timing, NOIs, Adverse and HMDA. Review data was aggregated to reports for management staff.

LWC Document Manager

 

A custom Salesforce File Manager built using LWC and AWS S3. Integrated PDF document viewer via PDF.js. Features included basic file upload, download, viewing, archiving, tagging, and moving.

Approval Discrepancy Tracking Form

 

A custom form allowing underwriting and support staff to have real-time insight into loan term changes after the intial or final approval. Catching these discrepancies early insures no delays when loans are ready for docs and funding.
